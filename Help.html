Help


1. How do I add a block?

Go to the Add Block page. From there, enter in the information you want to store on a block and click mine. A transaction window will appear from Metamask asking you to confirm the transaction. Hit the submit button and your block will be sent to the blockchain to be mined. Once it is mined, it'll be displayed to you on the page.

2. How do I view a block?

To view the blocks on your blockchain, make sure you are logged into Metamask and click the View the Blockchain - Advanced or Ledger page.

3: What are the Blockchain Statistics?

Blockchain Statistics consists of all of the nodes on the blockchain network as well as information about how many blocks are on the blockchain, when the last block was last added, the gas price and limit, etc.

4: How do I search for a block?

To search for a block, just enter in the parameters (name, id, or age) and click Search.
