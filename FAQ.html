FAQ

Q: What type of encryption is used by a blockchain?

A: SHA256 (Secure Hash Algorithm)

Q: What is a blockchain?

A: A blockchain is a decentralized and distributed ledger that allows for recording any kind of data.

Q: What is Metamask?

A: Metamask is the bridge that allows for communication between our web application and our private blockchain.

Q: What is GETH?

A: GETH allows us to run a private Ethereum blockchain.

Q: What is TestRPC?

A: TestRPC allows us to simulate a private blockchain. It performs all of the operations that a blockchain does, but it isn't a real blockchain structure - just a simulator.
