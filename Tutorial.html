Welcome to the Blockchain UI!

Let's get started. With this application, you can add a block to our blockchain, view the blocks, search for a specific block, and view statistics about the blockchain. First, we'll show you the Adding a Block page.

Adding a Block

To add a block, simply type in a name, age, and ID, and click "Mine." Your block will be mined to the blockchain. Let's continue to Searching for that block.

Searching for a Block

To search for a block, type in the transaction hash of the block you want to find and click "Submit". Let's continue to Viewing the Blockchain.

Viewing the Blockchain

On this page, all blocks from the blockchain will be displayed to you. Let's continue to Blockchain Statistics.

Viewing Blockchain Statistics

This page shows you the statistics about your blockchain, such as the number of blocks, when the last block was added, and the average time it takes to add a block to the blockchain.

Click exit to finish the tutorial.
